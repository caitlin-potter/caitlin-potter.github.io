Learning Plan

6 October 2019

Bootcamp

What do you think your biggest strengths and limitations will be in Bootcamp?

I hope that a strength of mine during bootcamp will be that I can collaborate well with others in my cohort. In terms of my weaknesses I think that I will struggle to speak up when it comes to group situations. I often struggle to communicate and explain things in a clear way, especially if it is a concept that I don't fully understand. Presenting is also not a strong point of mine, so having to practice this will help build up my confidence.

What do you think your biggest non-technical challenge at Bootcamp will be?

I think my biggest non-technical challenge at Bootcamp will be not to take things too personally and to not always compare my learning to others.

What are your expectations from the Bootcamp team?

I expect to learn in a friendly environment as well as be pushed out of my comfort zone daily. I also expect to receive feedback on the work I am doing.

What are your expectations of yourself on Bootcamp

While on bootcamp I will try and put in as much effort as I can. If I don't understand something I will try to put more time in to it outside of Bootcamp hours until I have a better understanding. I want to make sure that I enjoy my time on Bootcamp as well. I need to remember that it is such a short course and that I need to make the most of the experience.

Foundations

What is your long term goal and or career pathway?

My medium term goal is to change career paths and move in to tech, by becoming a web developer. I would love to be in a career that I enjoy and also in a working environment where I can continue to grow and develop my skills, but have have fun at the same time. I do enjoy working in areas that involve public and community health, so ultimately I would like to combine my new web development skills with a role that is helping out the community in terms of health in some way.

What skills would you like to see developed in yourself while at EDA?

Firstly, I would like to learn the technical skills well enough to move in to, and do well, in a web development role. The main human skills I would like to develop are to improve my communication skills (especially when working in teams) and also to learn how to give and receive feedback well.

A description of your own strengths and limitations

In terms of my strengths, I believe I am supportive, reliable and patient, which helps me work well in teams. Although these strengths often help me work in teams, I often take things too personally, internalise my feelings and struggle to ask for help, which lead me to stand back in group situations.

How will you manage your workload in this programme?

I will manage my workload during foundations by working from the EDA space as often as I can, and by making sure I set goals, plan my day well and most importantly take breaks when I need to. If I don't set goals for the day, I struggle to stay focused and keep on track.

How and where will you seek help in a timely way?

I will do my best to figure out how to solve a problem myself first. Although it is important for me to figure things out myself first, I've realised that I do struggle to ask for help, as I want to be able to figure things out for myself. This isn't always effective as it means that I sit there struggling for longer, ultimately wasting time, when I should just reach out to my peers. I need to keep reminding myself that we are all on a learning journey together, and it's fine if it takes me longer than others.

What do you expect from the facilitation team?

I expect support and guidance from the facilitation team where necessary, so I know if I am on the right track. The team has been great so far, and have already provided me with support and guidance. I would also appreciate any feedback that will help me grow both my technical and human skills.
